About Me

I am a highly motivated researcher, I have experience in data analysis, data science, and optimization. I have completed a Ph.D. in computer science. In my research, I try to solve the various problems encountered in the field of biometrics using bio-inspired artificial intelligence (AI) methods. In other words, I modeled problems encountered in biometrics as optimization problems. Such as the enhancement of the biometrics images, the selection, reduction, and the fusion of biometrics data. I am interested in working on interdisciplinary topics where I will use my background and experience in computer science, data analysis and data science to contribute to the analysis, prediction and the development of new techniques to make informed decisions.

Keywords: Data analysis, Data science, Machine learning, Artificial intelligence, pattern recognition.

Tools: Python, SQL, Power Bi, Tableau, Matlab, Latex.
